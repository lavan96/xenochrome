1. Project Overview:

APACS Consultation provides strategic business advisory, renovation project planning, and first-hand car dealership services, with inquiry-based listings for second-hand and reconditioned vehicles. We delivered a clean, light-themed site with bright-yellow accents and a powerful custom admin dashboard to manage all three verticals.

2. Client Objectives:

  • Showcase Service Verticals

    Clearly present Business Portfolio, Renovation Services, and First-Hand Car Dealership offerings.
  • Request-Based Car Listings

    Allow customers to browse second-hand and recon vehicles and submit inquiry forms.
  • Custom Admin Dashboard

    Enable APACS staff to upload and manage consultation projects, renovation jobs, and car inventory with visibility toggles.
  • Lead Capture

    Streamline enquiry forms for consultations, renovation quotes, and car requests.
  • Brand Consistency

    Use a light, minimalist UI with bright yellow highlights to reinforce APACS’s energetic, forward-thinking identity.

3. Key Challenges:

  • Multiple Verticals

    Three distinct service types each require their own presentation and management workflows.
  • No E-commerce Flow

    Cars aren’t sold directly online—listings must drive requests and follow-up.
  • Admin Flexibility

    Off-the-shelf CMSes couldn’t provide the granular controls (category management, toggles) APACS needed.
  • Performance & Responsiveness

    High-resolution project images and dynamic listings demanded optimized loading on all devices.

4. Our Solution:

4.1 Information Architecture:

  • Hero Banner

    Full-width tagline “Empower Your Business, Renovate Your Space, Drive Your Future” atop a bright-yellow gradient overlay..
  • Service Sections

    • Business Portfolio: Card grid with project thumbnails and “Learn More” links.
    • Renovation Services: Before/after slider galleries and quote request form.
    • Car Dealership: First-hand inventory listing; second-hand & recon inquiry forms.
  • Enquiry Forms

    Contextual forms embedded in each section, driving leads into Supabase.
  • Footer

    Quick links, contact info, and social icons.

4.2 Visual & UX:

  • Light Minimalist Theme

    Crisp white backgrounds with charcoal text, punctuated by bright-yellow buttons and hover states.
  • Responsive Grid & Listings

    Flexbox and CSS Grid for seamless adaptation—three-column layout on desktop collapsing to single column on mobile.
  • Interactive Elements

    Subtle hover animations on cards, inline form validation, and smooth scroll-to-section links.

4.3 Admin Dashboard & User Features:

  • Custom Admin Console

    • Business Portfolio Management: Add/edit projects with title, description, images, and publish toggle.
    • Renovation Services Management: Upload before/after photos, assign service categories, and enable/disable offerings.
    • Car Dealership Management:
      • First-Hand Cars: Add/edit projects with title, description, images, and publish toggle.
      • Second-Hand & Recon Cars: View incoming requests, update availability, and respond directly from the dashboard..
  • Role-Based Access

    Secure login for admins with granular permissions per vertical.

4.4 Technical Stack:

  • Frontend

    Vite.js + Next.js (React) for fast builds & server-side rendering
  • Backend & Auth

    Supabase for database, authentication, and real-time data
  • Hosting & CDN

    Vercel for global edge deployment
  • Styling & Utilities

    Tailwind CSS, PostCSS, and Headless UI for consistent component styling
  • Forms & Analytics

    Supabase-triggered emails and Google Analytics integration

5. Results & Impact:

  • 60% Increase in form submissions within two months
  • Admin Efficiency—manage new entries in under 5 minutes
  • 40% Faster page loads via image optimization
  • 75% Lift in time-on-page for service details

6. Next Steps:

  • CRM Integration: Sync enquiry data with a CRM for automated follow-up sequences.
  • Client Portal: Build a secure customer login area to track request statuses.
  • Multilingual Support: Add Malay and English toggles to broaden regional reach.
