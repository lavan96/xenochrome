Bartending without Boundaries


We bring the bar to you


A professional, fully insured bartending company in Klang Valley, that offers you and your guests a one-of-a-kind bartending experience. We serve weddings, special & corporate events with our portable bars and other unique bar rental options. We specialize in signature craft cocktails using seasonal and fresh ingredients and are committed to providing you and your guests a custom & memorable experience that will be talked about for days and toasted to for years to come!
