Meet Our CEO: Lavanethaan Ravachandran

Biography

Lavanethaan Ravachandran is the visionary founder and CEO of Xenochrome Technologies. Holding a Bachelor’s in Business Administration (Honours) and an HRDC-accredited trainer certification, he brings extensive experience in digital marketing, web development, and data science to every project. Under his guidance, Xenochrome has delivered tailored AI-driven automation solutions across healthcare, education, hospitality, and finance.

Leadership Philosophy

Lavan believes in people-centric innovation. He fosters a culture of continuous learning, collaboration, and integrity. His hands-on approach ensures that every solution is tailored to client needs, delivering measurable impact and sustainable growth.

Vision & Mission

As CEO, Lavan’s mission is to position Xenochrome at the forefront of AI-driven automation, empowering businesses to harness the full potential of intelligent technologies. He envisions a future where seamless automation elevates human creativity and operational excellence.
